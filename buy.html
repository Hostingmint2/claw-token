How it works

  1. Buy SOL (fees) or USDC (stable) using a card on a regulated on-ramp.
  2. Send it to your own Solana wallet (Phantom, etc.).
  3. Swap SOL/USDC → CLAW using a DEX aggregator.

OPSEC note: card payments reduce anonymity. For high-risk users, prefer Tor Browser, avoid reusing wallets tied to your identity, and don’t mix browsing/profiles.
